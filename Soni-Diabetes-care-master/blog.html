FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

1.WHAT IS DIABETES?

DIABETES MELLITUS MEANS HIGH BLOOD SUGAR. INSULIN HORMONE IN BODY CONTROLS SUGAR IN BLOOD. IN TYPE 1 DIABETES, OUR BODY FAILS TO PRODUCE THIS INSULIN HORMONE WHICH RESULTS IN HIGH BLOOD SUGAR, THIS TYPE OF DIABETIC PATIENTS NEEDS LIFELONG INSULIN INJECTIONS, NO OTHER OPTIONS AVAILABLE TILL PRESENT TIME. WHAT HAPPENS IN TYPE 2 DIABETES, MECHANISM IS MORE COMPLEX. THIS TYPE OF HIGH BLOOD SUGAR IS MAINLY A LIFESTYLE DISEASE, MEANS DUE TO SOME METABOLIC DERANGEMENTS INSULIN HORMONE DOES NOT WORK PROPERLY (MEANS INSULIN RESISTANCE). IN EARLY PHASE OF TYPE 2 DIABETES (KNOWN AS PREDIABETES PHASE) YOUR PANCREAS TRIES TO PRODUCE MORE INSULIN TO CONTROL YOUR BLOOD SUGAR TO OVERCOME THE RESISTANCE BUT AFTER 3-5 YEARS OF THIS STAGE, YOUR OVERBURDENED PANCREAS BECAME TIRED AND FAILS TO PRODUCE ENOUGH AMOUNT OF INSULIN AND YOUR BLOOD SUGAR STARTED TO RISE NOW.

2.CAUSE OF DIABETES?

TYPE 1 DIABETES IS UNFORTUNATELY AN AUTO-IMMUNE DISEASE, ANTIBODY KILLS YOUR BETA CELLS WHICH PRODUCE INSULIN HORMONE IN OUR BODY. TYPE 2 DIABETES HAS A LIFESTYLE FACTORS, OBESITY, BAD DIETARY HABITS AND POOR PHYSICAL ACTIVITY INCREASES INSULIN RESISTANCE IN BODY.

3.HOW TO RECOGNISE DIABETES?

DIABETIC PATIENT MAY FEEL WEAKNESS, INCREASED THIRST OR APPETITE, HIGH URINE OUTPUT AND POOR WOUND HEALING BUT IN MORE THAN 50% OF PATIENTS THESE SYMPTOMS DOESNOT APPEAR AND PATIENT MAY REMAIN UNDIAGNOSED FOR YEARS BEFORE DIAGNOSIS. SO, IF YOU ARE HAVING ANY ONE RISK FACTOR FOR TYPE 2 DM. GET YOUR BLOOD SUGAR TEST ONCE IN EVERY YEAR EVEN IN ABSENCE OF ANY DIABETIC SYMPTOMS.

4.WHY SHOULD I TAKE TREATMENT OF DIABETES EVEN I DON'T HAVE ANY SYMPTOMS?

WHEN SUGAR IS HIGH IN OUR BLOOD, OUR BLOOD VESSELS EXPOSE TO THIS HIGH LEVEL OF SUGAR FOR LONGER TIME WHICH RESULTS IN DAMAGE TO THESE BLOOD VESSELS (MECHANISM IS COMPLEX) WHICH ULTIMATELY LEADS TO SMALL THROMBUS AND OCCLUSION OF THESE BLOOD VESSELS CAUSING DAMAGE TO VARIOUS ORGANS LIKE KIDNEY RETINA HEART BRAIN ETC. AS YOU KNOW SYMPTOMS DOES NOT OCCURS IN EVERY PATIENT BUT IF SUGAR IS NOT CONTROLLED COMPLICATION OCCURS IN EACH AND EVERY PATIENT

5.CAN DIABETES BE CURED WITH TREATMENT?

NO, AND EVEN NOT POSSIBLE IN NEAR FUTURE BECAUSE FOR PERMANENT CURE, WE HAVE TO TRANSPLANT BETA-CELLS. TILL TODAY FROM ALL AVAILABLE CURRENT GUIDELINES FOR MANAGEMENT OF DIABETES, WE CAN CONTROL THE SUGAR IN BLOOD AND PREVENT THESE FUTURE COMPLICATIONS VERY EASILY AND SAFELY YOU CAN LIVE A HEALTHY AND QUALITY LIFE WITH DIABETES, BUT YOUR INITIAL YEARS AFTER DIAGNOSIS IS MOST CRUCIAL AND ALWAYS REQUIRES SPECIAL CARE. PROPER DIETARY ADVICE... RIGHT MEDICATION TO CONTROL YOUR SUGAR SO BETA CELL FUNCTION CAN IMPROVE AND YEARLY MONITORING OF COMPLICATIONS, SO WE CAN TREAT THEM EARLY.

6.WHY SHOULD I TAKE TREATMENT IF CURE IS NOT POSSIBLE ?

UNCONTROLLED BLOOD SUGAR IS VERY DANGEROUS FOR OUR BLOOD VESSELS, CAN DAMAGE THEM RESULTS IN THROMBOSIS (BLOOD CLOT) IN EVERY TYPE OF BLOOD VESSELS. THIS OCCLUSION OF BLOOD SUPPLY IS VERY HARMFUL IN LONGER DURATION.

7.WHAT IS TREATMENT OPTIONS FOR DIABETES?

DIET IS MOST IMPORTANT, 2ND THING IS PHYSICAL ACTIVITY AFTER THAT DRUGS COMES. FOR TYPE 1 DIABETES INSULIN IS ONLY AVAILABLE TREATMENT BUT IN CASE OF TYPE 2 DIABETES ORAL DRUGS ARE EFFECTIVE IN CONTROLLING BLOOD SUGAR WITH PROPER GUIDANCE.

8.WHAT IS ROLE OF INSULIN IN DIABETES?

FOR A DIABETES PATIENT INSULIN IS LIFE. IN TYPE 1 DIABETES, REGULAR INSULIN INJECTIONS WITH PROPER GUIDANCE IS THE ONLY BEST AVAILABLE TREATMENT. FOR TYPE 2 PATIENT AS MAIN MECHANISM IS INSULIN RESISTANCE AND ORAL DRUGS ARE SO EFFECTIVE BUT THERE IS ALWAYS POOR BETA CELLS FUNCTION AT THE TIME OF DIAGNOSIS, AND REMAIN FOREVER SO TIME TO TIME IN TYPE 2 DIABETES PATIENT REQUIRES INSULIN UNDER SPECIALISED DIABETES CARE.

9. WHAT IS BENEFIT OF INSULIN WORKSHOP

INSULIN EDUCATION IS MUST FOR EVERY DIABETIC WHO REQUIRES INSULIN. DIFFERENT INSULINS HAVE DIFFERENT DURATION OF ACTION. WHICH INSULIN, WHEN AND HOW TO INJECT IS VERY IMPORTANT DURING INSULIN THERAPY.

10.DIET FOR A DIABETIC PATIENT?

MOST FREQUENT QUESTION FROM A DIABETIC PATIENT IS WHAT TO EAT. ANSWER IS NOT SO EASY BECAUSE DIET IS INDIVIDUALISED, DEPENDS ON SO MANY FACTORS LIKE PERSONAL HABITS, SOCIAL AND CULTURAL STATUS AND MANY MORE. DIET IS NOT LIKE DRUGS THAT A WRITTEN DIET FORMAT GIVEN TO PATIENT AT THE TIME OF DIAGNOSIS IS COMPLETE. EVERY PATIENT NEEDS TO KNOW ABOUT NUTRITION AND DIET EDUCATION IN HIS OWN LANGUAGE AND UNDERSTANDING.
